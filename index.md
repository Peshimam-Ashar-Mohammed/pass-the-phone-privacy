Privacy Policy for Pass the Phone
Pass the Phone ("the app") is a casual party game designed for entertainment purposes.
Information Collection and Use
The app does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Advertising
The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use device information such as advertising ID, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policies.
We do not have access to or control over the data collected by Google AdMob.

Children’s Information
The app is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services
The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for more information:
https://policies.google.com/privacy
